01_discover

Impulse purchases represent almost 40% of all the money spent on e-commerce. Moment App gives you opportunity to stop and be aware of your own impulse. It asks you a few questions to help you discover, why you (don’t) need this thing in your closet/home/life. No more guilt – take control of your spending with Moment.

02_define
